# 570. Managers with at Least 5 Direct Reports

```mysql
SELECT
    e.name
FROM
    Employee e
JOIN (
    SELECT
        e1.id,
        COUNT(e1.id) AS 'managerTimes'
    FROM
        Employee e1
    JOIN Employee e2 ON
        e1.id = e2.managerId
    GROUP BY e1.id
) c ON 
    e.id = c.id
WHERE
    c.managerTimes >= 5;
```
